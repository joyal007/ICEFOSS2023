ICEFOSS 2023

ICEFOSS 2023 is a National level Free Software Conference organized jointly by FISAT Free Software Cell (FFSC) and ACM Student Chapter, FISAT . The conference is open to all FOSS enthusiasts and interested individuals. Scheduled to take place from April 2nd to 4th, ICEFOSS 2023 provides a platform for attendees to learn, share and network with like-minded individuals.

What's HACKFIT?

HACKFIT is a 36-hour Hackathon conducted as part of ICEFOSS 2023 commencing from the 2nd of April 2023 to the 4th of April 2023. Participants can enter the competition individually or as a team of not more than 5 members. The Hackathon will have 2 rounds with round 1 being exclusively online. The winning team along with the 2 runners up teams will be offered an opportunity to connect with some of our leading industry partners.

Problem Statement for Hackathon

A good hackathon problem statement sparks creativity, provokes unconventional thinking, and empowers teams to change the world.

  • FOOD SAFETY
  • WILD ANIMAL DETECTION

Food safety measures include hygiene practices such as hand-washing, using clean utensils and equipment, and maintaining a clean and sanitary environment. Food handlers are also required to follow proper food handling and cooking techniques to prevent the spread of bacteria and other harmful microorganisms.


To ensure food safety, many governments and regulatory bodies around the world have developed food safety standards and guidelines that must be followed by food businesses and producers. These standards cover a range of areas including food labeling, packaging, storage, and transport, as well as the testing and inspection of food products.


It is important for consumers to be aware of food safety and to take measures to protect themselves and their families. This can include washing fruits and vegetables before consumption, cooking meat thoroughly, and storing food properly to prevent contamination. By following these practices, we can help to prevent foodborne illness and ensure the safety and quality of the food we consume.


As the number of restaurants closing down is increasing, due to lack of cleanliness and use of outdated ingredients, we need to find solutions for the same. This is an opportunity to come with a chance to develop a solution for food safety improvement.


Wild animal detection is an important aspect of life in many parts of the world, especially in areas where humans and wildlife regularly come into contact. There are a number of reasons why it may be necessary to detect the presence of wild animals, including for safety, conservation, and research purposes.


For safety purposes, it is important for hikers, campers, and others who may be spending time in areas with wild animals to be aware of their surroundings and to take precautions such as carrying bear spray or other deterrents. In some cases, trained dogs can also be used to detect the presence of certain types of animals, such as invasive species or those that may be causing damage to crops or other resources.


Overall, wild animal detection is an important part of life in many parts of the world, and plays a critical role in ensuring the safety and well-being of both humans and wildlife. You can provide a technological solution for wildlife detection which will secure human life in the surrounding areas.
